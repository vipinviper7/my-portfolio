About Me

I'm Vipin — a Visual Designer currently at Zoho, where I work across both marketing and product.

I've shaped marketing visuals for products like Catalyst, Marketplace, and Zoho Affiliate — and designed product experiences for Zoho Sprints. Whether it's a campaign creative or a feature interface, I care about making things clear, useful, and well-crafted.

My path into design wasn't linear. I started out as a service engineer, moved into graphic design, and gradually found my way to product and UX. Along the way, I was part of Cohort 11 at 10kdesigners, which helped sharpen the way I think about and practice design. Every role and experience taught me to look at problems differently — and that's what keeps me going.
