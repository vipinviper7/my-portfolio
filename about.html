Let's Talk

I design products people don't need a tutorial for.

I'm Vipin — a Product Designer with 4 years of hands-on product design experience and 6 years in the design industry, focused on building user-centered digital products using AI, research, and data-driven design.

Currently at Zoho, I lead end-to-end product design for Zoho Sprints — owning the UX across core features from research and wireframing through to high-fidelity delivery. I also designed and shipped an illustration system and icon library for Sprints, and extended my scope to marketing and visual design for Catalyst, Marketplace, and Zoho Affiliate.

My path into design wasn't linear. I started as a 2D graphic designer at Eve Spark, moved into visual design at IMC Group, and found my way to product and UX at Zoho. I'm a 10k Designers C11 graduate (Designer in Residence) and UX Mint certified. Every role sharpened a different skill — and now I bring all of them to the table.
